Why hackX?

The Most successful inter University hackathon, has arrived it's 4th chapter - hackX 2019, addressing the innovative youth of Sri Lankan universities to turn their Ideas into Reality. hackX 2019 takes the traditional Inter-University Hackathon culture a step further by allowing the Innovative Ideas to be implemented in a competitive business environment. hackX 2019 revamps the ordinary hackathon into an Innovative Startup Battle.

Things to Remember

All Teams must comprise of minimum 3 up to 5 Members.

All participants must be Internal students of a State University or a Higher Educational Institute.

A Team should have members from the Same University or Institute.

All proposals must abide by the given proposal guidelines and the given Proposal Structure.

All Ideas should be NOVEL and related to Information Technology Aspects in the given categories.

TIMELINE

AUGUST 25th

hackX Ideation - initial pitch

@ Department of industrial Management

30 best teams among all applicants will be selected as Semi-Finalists and invited to pitch their Initial Idea in 5 Minutes.

AUGUST 1st - 21st

Registration

@ via hackx.lk

Once the Registration is opened, download the sample Proposal Strcture, complete it and submit with your team details.

SEPTEMBER 4th - 11th

Intermediate Webinars

@ via Livestream

Intermediate Technical webinar session will be officialy launched. Sessions will be broadcasted live through the Social Media.

SEPTEMBER 1st

Business Workshop

@ hatch. works

10 finalists out of 30 teams will be selcted by their Initial pitch. Hackthon will be started for those, including a Business Seminar as the first workshop.

SEPTEMBER 22nd

hackX finals

@ Dialog Axiata Auditorium

'hackX finals' will be the final platform for the final 10 teams to demostrate thier 3 week mastered production and battle for the prizes.

SEPTEMBER 8th

Technical Workshop

@ hatch. works

A technical workshop will be conducted for final 10 teams on product development stages, prototyping and recent technology trends.
